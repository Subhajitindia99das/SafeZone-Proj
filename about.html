FAQ

Does it need a smartphone?
No. The device can transmit on its own. A phone app is optional for family notifications.
What happens after an SOS?
Location is captured, the device identity is verified, the alert is prioritized, and the nearest police station is notified for dispatch.
How is privacy protected?
Data is encrypted; only essential info is shared with responders. There is no continuous tracking unless an SOS is active or a short heartbeat is configured.
